Self Reflection

Three Areas of Quality Work

One area where I think I produced quality work was creating a unigue and well designed global navigation bar. Another area where I believe I produced quality work was the homepage because I think that it displays exactly what the site has to offer content wise. The homepage is also easy to use and well styled. A third area of quality work, in my opinion, is that a lot of attention was put into ensuring the mobile site was well structured and not "broken".

A-Level Work

The areas where I believe I displayed A-Level work were in the overall layout of the individual webpages, an innovative design for the homepage as well as trying to make the homepage look different but still look pleasing to the eye and lastly, ensuring that the flow of the page was concise and not sporadic.

Improvement For Next Time

One note for improvement would be the styling of the content within each white box on each content page. Another element that could be improved is the placement of images on the mobile site as well as the menu on the mobile site.

Elements of the Web Page

Unique Graphic

The graphic that was made from scratch on this website is located on the left of every title on every page.

Embedded Media

The embedded media that was used on my site were several videos taken from Youtube and they are located on the Map Reading & Navigation page, Range Estimation & Scope Usage page, Tactical Formations page, and the Combat Strategies page.

CSS Rollover Effect

Every image on the global navigation bar has a CSS rollover effect going from greyscale to full color when you hover over the image.
